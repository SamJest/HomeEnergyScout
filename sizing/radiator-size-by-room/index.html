Sizing guide

Radiator size by room

Use room size, room type and heat-loss clues as the starting point. Copying the radiator in the next room often leads to underheating or a room that only feels comfortable after running the system for too long.

Last reviewed: 16 April 2026 - Practical sizing guide, not an installer heat-loss design

Small bedroom

Often modest radiator output is enough, especially in newer or better insulated homes.

Average living room

Needs climb faster with larger windows, exposed walls and higher ceilings.

Cold extension

This is where rough rules are most likely to undershoot. Treat quick estimates with care.
